About Me☺

Hey y'all. My name is Krystal Long and I'm 17 years old. My pronouns are she/her and I identify with Chinese-Vietnamese culture. The only languages that I can speak fluently include Cantonese and English. I am the youngest and only daughter of three, born to immigrant parents. When I'm not in school, you can find me spending time alone at home, hanging out with my friends and/or family, or attending Key Club obligations. Regarding my personality, I'm an empathetic ambivert who has a certain weird humor that not everyone understands. To be frank, I'm not really studious, but I like to believe that I am smart since I'm able to maintain good grades. I am currently a senior at Oakland Technical High School and a Lifecycle Marketing Intern at Okta who has plans to attend a four-year college after graduating!
